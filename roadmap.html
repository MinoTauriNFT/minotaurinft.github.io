Leverage comes to NFTs

MinoTauris will be the first collection to allow its Holders to leverage their NFTs.

It will work as a loan, in which our holders will have the option to receive liquidity by blocking their NFT in a wallet that will be opened by the Team. They will be able to receive from 30%-80% of the value of their NFT in Stacks depending on the volatility of the market.

After repaying the amount borrowed in Stacks you will be able to unlock your NFT and it will be transferred back to your wallet. A small fee will be charged each time you use this system, to contribute to the liquidity providers and also to the Byzantion team.

The pricing of the NFTs will consist of a mathematical model that will take into account the 25 NFTs listed in the floor and will also include some variability according to traits.

In the event that your NFT should go down in value, the mechanism will liquidate your position with a margin of 3-7% of the Stacks lent to the Holder.

The NFT would be listed by the mechanism directly at floor price in order for the fund to recover the liquidity.
