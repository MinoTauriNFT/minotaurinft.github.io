Something unique comes to Stacks

MinoTauri will be available for minting to the public, will you be lucky enough?

The vision of the team is clear, this is a long term project even though most of the points of the roadmap that we present to you are already designed and those that are not, are already in their beta phase.

We have no doubt that the Metaverse and WEB3 are the present and the future of technology.

For us it is essential to be transparent with our holders, that is why from minute 1 the project will be fully audited. The launch date will be announced as soon as the project's quality and technical aspects are fully ready.

An accelerator + launchpad for NFT projects on Stacks

The team is formed by people in love with the Stacks ecosystem, we are convinced that Stacks in the not too distant future will be a reference in development and creation of NFTs, in fact it already is by fundamentals and development, but people need to know and learn the main features that make Stacks different from other projects and when they get to know us they will not hesitate to join.

The team's goal is to accelerate this transition by attracting talent to Stacks, both developers and project creators.

We will invest in the creation of projects in which we will participate as shareholders and our holders will be able to be part of the board members and all the owners of MinoTauris will be remunerated with the profits generated by the projects in which we invest.

Finally, we will dedicate 5% of the mint funds to training new developers for Stacks, providing all the necessary tools and the best professionals at zero cost. and last, but not least, we will promote community education on Stacks.

Boring merchandising is forbidden

We are taking merchandising to another level. Merchandise that not only looks great but provides functionality to help one along their journey.

Don't be surprised if there are amazing collaborations with different Stacks projects and all the merchandising will be from the best brands.

Holders will be able to redeem a free merchandising pack every 6 months.

Sneak peek: Merchandising will have utilities in the metaverse.

Access to our Ethereum journey

All our holders will have access to the Ethereum collection which will allow us to open horizons into the metaverse.

The MinoTauris team aims to open bridges to ETH and SOL in order to expand the knowledge about Stacks and therefore the liquidity coming to Stacks.

MinoTauri will always be the gateway to ALL the benefits of our developments and profits.

We go deep into the Metaverse

The metaverse is already the present and future of social connections thanks to the rapid development of WEB3. We want to be part of a site where you can share experiences with other users of the community, where you can buy properties, where going to work is much easier.

Leverage comes to NFTs

We have all heard of the famous and dangerous "Leverage".

MinoTauris will be the first collection to allow its Holders to leverage their NFTs.

The team has developed a mathematical model that is already in Beta phase that will allow the absorption of a concept that seemed impossible to apply to NFTs.

The model is based on the floor price and the traits contained in each NFT, our holders will be able to make Longs and Shorts to our project and hopefully in a few months we will be able to implement it to different projects of the Stacks ecosystem from being the owner of a MinoTauri.
